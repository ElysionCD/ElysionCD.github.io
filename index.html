ABOUT THE GAME

Asterion Quest is an 8-bit side-scrolling action platformer with classic retro mechanics and linear level design. Take control of Asterios, the Minotaur, and explore the dangerous Forbidden Continent in a quest for his adventure companion.

FEATURES

  • 6 levels of classic linear design.
  • 8-bit style graphics, based on the NES palette.
  • Pure old school action!
  • +10 different weapons, equipment and items to collect!
  • Huge bosses!
  • In-game secrets and alternative endings.
  • Death counter.
  • New Game+.
  • Extras, including Boss Rush mode, Speed Run mode, Bestiary and Sound Test!
